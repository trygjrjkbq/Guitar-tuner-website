Microcontroler ESP32

The ESP-WROOM-32 microcontroller controls our tuner. It receives the signal from the microphone, calculates the correct frequency, and sends the information to the display. It also includes WiFi and Bluetooth modules to connect to the webpage. The microcontroller has 30 GPIO pins, which makes it easier to connect other components.

Display TFT LCD ST7735

The ST7735 display is 1.8 inches in size and has a resolution of 128 Ã— 160 pixels. It can show different colors and shapes in a very readable and attractive way. The display requires a 3.3 V power supply, which is perfect for our microcontroller.

Microphone MAX4466

Our microphone requires a supply voltage between 2.4 V and 5.5 V, which is suitable because the ESP provides 3.3 V to the MAX4466. The microphone amplifier has a Power Supply Rejection Ratio of 112 dB, meaning the signal experiences less distortion from power supply better.
